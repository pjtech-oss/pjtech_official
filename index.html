Together, these metrics empower businesses to make informed decisions, streamline operations, and enhance overall performance.

At PJTech POS, we offer a comprehensive suite of analytics to empower your business with actionable insights. Our analytics capabilities cover key areas including sales, inventory, payroll, and transaction data.
Sales Analytics: Gain deep insights into your sales performance, including trends, peak hours, top-selling products, and customer behavior patterns. This helps you optimize pricing, promotions, and inventory management strategies.
Inventory Analytics: Track inventory levels in real-time, monitor stock movement, identify fast-moving and slow-moving items, and forecast demand accurately. This enables you to minimize stockouts, reduce excess inventory costs, and improve overall inventory efficiency.
Payroll Analytics: Manage labor costs effectively by analyzing payroll data such as wages, hours worked, overtime, and employee productivity metrics. This helps in budgeting accurately and optimizing staffing levels based on business needs.
Transaction Analytics: Monitor transaction data to identify transaction volumes, average ticket size, payment methods used, and sales trends over time. This information allows you to understand customer spending habits and tailor marketing and promotional strategies accordingly.

Our analytics tools are designed to provide you with actionable insights that drive informed decision-making, improve operational efficiency, and ultimately enhance your business performance.
